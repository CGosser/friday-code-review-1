About me

A bit of background

I am fairly new to web development, but I learn quickly and as an end user I have made a bit of a hobby of experiencing the best and worst of web design. I personally enjoy Brutalist web design, although I am aware that lighter and softer websites are more pleasant for every day use. I have experience working for business process outsourcing companies where worked in the accounts of a number of major companies that you may own the products of yourself, but which I cannot disclose the names of due to non disclosure agreements. I have some limited experience with Python, in addition to the languages we are learning here.
